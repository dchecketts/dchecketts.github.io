Far from the first time that I overhauled the code that's made up my site, this time I've switched over to [Astro](https://astro.build/), in addition to sticking with [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/) & [HTMX](https://htmx.org/). My main goal for this site is to have one place where I can share projects that I've worked on, a little about myself, and just some fun things like this blog to share my thoughts with people. A few weeks ago, one of my brothers decided to get into programming, so I decided to add a "Learn" section where I'd include resources and kind of lesson things to get him more familiar with different aspects of programming.

This time, I've got a toolkit set up that I believe I'll be able to stick with for a while.
